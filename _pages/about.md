As an undergraduate researcher, I am currently working on designing a liquid metal printer utilizing stepper motor controls for position and syringe actuation. I received the NASA LaSPACE LURA award in 2025, and visited the Langley Research Center in January 2026.

I also enjoy using Blender for both personal and research graphic purposes. I have become interested in using its rigging and animation capabilities to control and mirror robotic elements.
